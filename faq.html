FAQ

Answers to frequently asked questions  asked by the users about TV mounting and wire hiding services

How does TV mounting work?

Our technicians can help mount your big screen TV on the wall with expertise, to make all the services faster for you, we have many locations worldwide. Our technicians are best with any TV(screen size), and we have almost every style of bracket available.

Does TV mounting services come with any guarantee?

Yes! All the installation services from our firm are backed by a 60-day guarantee.

How much time it will take to mount my TV?

On average, it takes around 40-60 minutes only to do the complete installation.

Do we install any brand of TV?

Yes, we support all brands TVs for mounting services.

What size TVs do we install?

We can install any TV size, tell your TV screen size to the technician after booking the appointment.

Do I need TV bracket also?

Our prices on the site or offers do not include the price for the bracket. Tell this thing to the technician, and they will bring a brand new bracket at your place.

If you already have a bracket for your TV, it's great! and in that case, we will do the installation with your old bracket itself.

What kind of TV brackets we offer?

Fixed, Tilting and Full motion brackets are the types of TV brackets that we offer, and they are best for any TV size.

What additional services do we provide?

There are many add-on services that you can select as part of your TV mounting. We can conceal your wires, program TV remote, sound bar installation, configure Xbox etc.

Is it possible to mount a TV on a wall if it's not drywall?

Yes ofcorse! Our skilled and experienced technicians can mount your TV on all types of walls, including drywall, wood, concrete, or brick.
